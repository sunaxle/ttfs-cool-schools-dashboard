The Hub of Prosperity

First planted in 2019 by UTRGV Agroecology and Edinburg First United Methodist Church, this community farm is blossoming into a regional hub for sustainable agriculture research and education.

Agriculture 01

  • Weekly farm stand, Saturdays Nov - June
  • Online sales via Sentli Center
  • Donations to FUMC and UTRGV food pantries
  • Volunteer for veggies!

Research 02

  • Regionally adapted vegetable varieties
  • Cover cropping and soil health
  • Low-input pest management
  • Natives in agroecosystems

Education 03

  • Monthly community workshops
  • UTRGV courses and tours
  • 30+ student employees, interns, and counting!
  • Community conversations on food system issues

Community 04

  • 20 bed community garden
  • 8 member incubator farm
  • Perimeter walking trail
  • Special events like Tomato Fest and RGV Food Summit
Welcome to the Hub - where the land loves you back!
