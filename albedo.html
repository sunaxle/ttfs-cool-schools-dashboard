Surface Material Impact

Albedo is the measure of how much solar radiation a surface reflects. Dark surfaces (asphalt) absorb heat, creating Urban Heat Islands. Light surfaces and vegetation reflect or dissipate it.

Campus Heat Island Risk

High

Large continuous expanses of asphalt increase ambient temperatures by 5-8°F.

Mitigation Strategies

  • Depave unused parking zones and replace with permeable pavers or grass.
  • Plant shade trees strategically to block afternoon sun on exposed concrete.
  • Apply highly reflective coatings to school rooftops.
